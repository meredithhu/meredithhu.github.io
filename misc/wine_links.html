NYC wine bars and stores --- my personal reviews:

  1. Aldo Sohm: excellent wine list, 9pm pours, and the somms there love to geek out with you!
  2. Terrior: by the one and only Paul Grieco, the menu/wine list is fun, informative, and excellent!
  3. La Compagnie des Vins Surnaturels: great wine list, fun blind tasting gimmick!
  4. Casellula: features wine and cheese - solid wine list, they appear to have quite some favorite producers, which show up in the list again and again and again --- they are fantastic producers for sure, but I would prefer more variety.
  5. The Ten Bells: with this glassware? Seriously? All natural wines. Not a fan despite the hype.
  6. Astor: the largest and the most comprehensive, region/type discounts every Tuesday/Wednesday/..., some mega tastings are awesome.
  7. Union Square Wine: smaller but a bit more boutique than Astors. I personally frequent their tasting events. Their Sake, Georgian, German, and California selections stand out to me.
  8. Flatiron Wine: smaller but even more boutique than Union Square. Their events are awesome.
  9. Sherry-Lehmann® Wines: solid selection overall, somewhere in-between UNS and Flatiron.
  10. Verve Wine: went for the Master Somm there...
